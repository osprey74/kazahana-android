# v3.2.1 — Store listing ("What's new")

Short release notes for the Google Play "What's new" field (under 500 characters per language).

## English

Bug fixes and UX improvements:
- The "New message" button on the Messages screen is now always visible (moved to the top bar).
- The new post button now appears only on the Home and Profile tabs.
- The evacuation alert banner no longer appears on the Settings and shelter navigation screens.
